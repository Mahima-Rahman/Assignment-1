Pillars of New Bangladesh

Innovation

Innovation is the key to success in the 21st century. Bangladesh is no exception. We need to innovate in every sector to make our country a developed one.

Sustainability

Sustainability the first step to innovation. We need to come up with new Sustainability solve the problems of our country. We need to think out of the box to make our country a better place.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is the ability to see the future. We need to have a clear Infrastructure of what we want to achieve as a country. We need to set our goals and work towards them to make our country a developed one.

Empowerment

Empowerment are the obstacles that we need to overcome to achieve our goals. We need to face the challenges that come our way and find solutions to them to make our country a better place.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness
